Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I place an order?

    • To place an order, simply select a book, add it to your cart, proceed to checkout, and fill in your delivery details. You can pay online through a secure payment system or choose cash on delivery (if available).

  • Can I return a book if it doesn't meet my expectations?

    • Yes, you can return a book within 14 days of purchase, as long as it is in its original condition and packaging. Please contact our customer support for detailed return instructions.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    • We accept payments via Visa, MasterCard, as well as through payment systems like PayPal. Some regions may also offer cash on delivery as an option.

  • How do I know if a book is in stock?

    • All available books on our website are marked as "in stock." If a book is temporarily unavailable, you can place a pre-order or get notified when the item is back in stock.

  • How can I stay updated on new books and discounts?

    • Subscribe to our newsletter or follow us on social media to stay informed about new arrivals, promotions, and discounts.

  • How can I contact you?

    • You can contact us through the contact form on our website or by phone. You can also email us at support@bookish.com.
